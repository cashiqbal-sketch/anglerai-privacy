AnglerAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

AnglerAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Camera & Photos: Used only to identify fish species. Photos are sent to Anthropic's AI for analysis and are not stored by us.

Location: Used to detect your state for fishing regulations and local weather. Never stored or shared.

Catch Log: Stored locally on your device only. We never upload or access this data.

Purchases: Handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Third-Party Services

Anthropic (fish identification), OpenWeatherMap (weather data), Apple StoreKit (subscriptions).
